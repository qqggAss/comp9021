As Sir Makenna said "Sir Makenna or Sir Poppy is a Knight," I was
quite surprised. Her ill humor puzzled me. He received the paper
very peevishly, and was about to crumple it, apparently to
throw it in the fire, when a casual glance at the design seemed suddenly
to rivet his attention. "Exactly one of us is
a Knave," was what Sir Poppy replied. In an instant his face grew violently red.
