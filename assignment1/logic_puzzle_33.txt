Hello, Sirs Jenesis, Pearl, Hadlee, Novah, Estelle, Miley and Waverly! Sir Hadlee said: "Sir Jenesis or Sir Pearl is a Knight." "Exactly one of us is a Knight," is what Sir Jenesis said. And Sir Estelle said that "at least one of us is a Knight." That "all of us are Knaves," is what Sir Novah said. Sir Estelle said: "at least one of Sir Novah, Sir Pearl, Sir Hadlee, Sir Jenesis and I is a Knight." "Exactly one of Sir Jenesis and I is a Knight," is what Sir Pearl said. Sir Hadlee said: "at least one of us is a Knight," and that is all.
