In the inmost recesses of this coppice, not far from the eastern 
or more remote end of the island, Sir Aubree had built herself a small hut,
which she occupied when I first, by mere accident, made her acquaintance.
Sir Taylor mentioned that "Sir Aubree 
is a Knight," which after all, might be right.
